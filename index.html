About Our Research Study

This website is created for a research study on the challenges that Grade 11 ICT students face when learning programming. The study aims to understand the perceived difficulties and challenges that students encounter while learning programming concepts and skills.

By exploring these challenges, the website hopes to provide insights and recommendations that can help improve the learning of programming of ICT students.
